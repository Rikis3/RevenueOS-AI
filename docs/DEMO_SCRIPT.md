# RevenueOS Live Demo Script

Use this script when presenting RevenueOS to a Hiring Manager or during a technical interview.

## 1. Introduction (The Hook)
"Hi team, thanks for having me. Today I want to walk you through RevenueOS. Most analytics portfolios are just Tableau dashboards showing 'what happened yesterday'. I built RevenueOS to show how modern RevOps teams should operate: by using AI and Data Engineering to tell us **why it happened** and **what to do tomorrow**."

## 2. The Command Center
"We're looking at the Executive Command Center. You'll notice there are no charts at the top. Why? Because the CEO doesn't want to hunt for insights. I built a Python backend using LangChain and DuckDB that reads the KPI data and generates this **AI Executive Briefing**. It forces the data to tell a story: here are our wins, here are our risks, and here is exactly what we need to do."

## 3. Revenue Risk Center
"If we click over to the Revenue Risk center, you'll see my account health model in action. I wrote a SQL model that analyzes SDR activity gaps and funnel leakage, and I feed that to a GPT-4 service that acts like Clari. It automatically flags that we have $4.2M at risk because Enterprise deals are stalling without activity."

## 4. Attribution Intelligence
"Finally, let's look at Marketing Attribution. Most candidates can build a First-Touch model. I built First-Touch, Last-Touch, Linear, and U-Shaped models in SQL, exposed them via FastAPI, and built this comparison UI in Next.js. I also built an AI narrative generator that explains the difference to a non-technical CMO—proving that webinars drive our mid-funnel velocity even if they don't generate the initial lead."

## 5. The Close
"In short, RevenueOS proves I can execute the full analytics lifecycle: from generating the raw data, to building the warehouse, engineering the SQL models, deploying the AI insights, and visualizing the business impact. I'm ready to bring this level of execution to your RevOps team on day one."
