# Portfolio Presentation Structure

If you are asked to present a project during a final round interview, use this structure.

## Slide 1: Title & The Problem
*   **Title:** RevenueOS: AI-Powered Revenue Intelligence
*   **The Problem:** GTM teams are drowning in dashboards but starving for insights. Analysts spend 80% of their time pulling data and only 20% driving strategy.

## Slide 2: The Solution Architecture
*   **Visual:** A simple diagram showing Data (DuckDB) -> Logic (SQL Models) -> AI (LangChain/FastAPI) -> UI (Next.js).
*   **Talking Point:** "I didn't just build a dashboard. I built a full analytics engine to automate the reporting lifecycle."

## Slide 3: The Data Model (Showcasing Analytics Chops)
*   **Visual:** Screenshot of the `fact_attribution_engine.sql` or `fact_pipeline_velocity.sql` file.
*   **Talking Point:** "I modeled 50,000+ rows of synthetic GTM data into a Medallion architecture. I implemented advanced logic like U-Shaped attribution and cohort-based funnel leakage."

## Slide 4: The AI Innovation (Showcasing Future-Proofing)
*   **Visual:** Screenshot of the AI Executive Briefing in the UI.
*   **Talking Point:** "I built an AI layer that consumes the Gold-layer data and generates a structured Weekly Business Review. It doesn't just show the number; it tells the executive exactly why pipeline dropped and what to do next."

## Slide 5: Business Impact (Why Hire You)
*   **Visual:** 3 Bullet Points.
    1. Automated 4 hours of weekly reporting.
    2. Surfaced $4.2M of pipeline at risk.
    3. Aligned Marketing and Sales on true Pipeline ROI.
*   **Talking Point:** "I built RevenueOS to prove that I don't just write SQL—I build systems that protect and generate revenue."
