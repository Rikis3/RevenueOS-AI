# Revenue Funnel Framework

This framework defines the exact lifecycle stages a prospect moves through, from initial marketing touchpoint to realized revenue. Standardizing these stages is required to measure Funnel Leakage and Pipeline Velocity.

## 1. Top of Funnel (Marketing Domain)

### Stage 1: Lead
*   **Definition**: A raw contact in the database. They may have downloaded a whitepaper or attended a webinar, but they have not been vetted.
*   **Exit Criteria**: Demographic and behavioral score reaches 50+, automatically promoting them to MQL.

### Stage 2: MQL (Marketing Qualified Lead)
*   **Definition**: A lead deemed "sales-ready" based on automated lead scoring (e.g., Director+ title, engaged with pricing page).
*   **Exit Criteria**: SDR accepts the lead and verifies contact information.

## 2. Mid Funnel (Sales Development Domain)

### Stage 3: SAL (Sales Accepted Lead) / Working
*   **Definition**: The SDR is actively calling/emailing the lead to book a discovery meeting.
*   **Exit Criteria**: Meeting booked and completed. Prospect demonstrates pain point and budget.

### Stage 4: SQL (Sales Qualified Lead)
*   **Definition**: The SDR has qualified the lead using BANT (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline). An Opportunity is officially created in the CRM and assigned to an Account Executive (AE).
*   **Exit Criteria**: AE conducts a Discovery call and confirms the opportunity is valid.

## 3. Bottom Funnel (Account Executive Domain)

### Stage 5: Discovery
*   **Definition**: Initial AE meeting to deep-dive into the customer's technical requirements and business pain.
*   **Probability to Close**: 10%

### Stage 6: Demo
*   **Definition**: AE and Sales Engineer present a tailored product demonstration proving the solution meets the requirements.
*   **Probability to Close**: 25%

### Stage 7: Proposal
*   **Definition**: Formal pricing and scope of work are delivered to the economic buyer.
*   **Probability to Close**: 50%

### Stage 8: Negotiation
*   **Definition**: Legal, security, and procurement reviews are actively occurring. Redlining the MSA.
*   **Probability to Close**: 75%

## 4. Resolution

### Stage 9A: Closed Won
*   **Definition**: Contract is signed. Deal value is booked as ARR.
*   **Probability to Close**: 100%

### Stage 9B: Closed Lost
*   **Definition**: The prospect chose a competitor, lost budget, or went dark.
*   **Probability to Close**: 0%
